Find target users.

People of which age download the App mostly. Everyone 10+, Teen and Mature 17+ have highest number of reviews compared with other groups. We should consider them as our potential target users if we want to build a popular app.

Find target users’ preferred category.

From the second graph, we can see the top 5 popular genres. We need to pay more attention on these genres if we want to create a propular App.

Detailed analysis of target users’ preferred category.

If the company want to develop an App which targets users are Mature 17+, they should consider News & Magazines, if they want their target users to be Everyone 10+, Game is the genre they want to consider, and if their target users are Teen, it’s better to choose Social genre.

Popularity and names length.

Tt’s better to give our App a name with short name with 1 or 2 letters, or 8 letters.

Correlation between Popularity and price.

Free apps definitely have tremendously more downloads compare with charged Apps. And most users purchased 5$ or less cost Apps. Free App and low price App is more popular.

Rating and popularity.

Users tend to try an App with high rating even without a lot of downloads. Users prefer higher rating Apps when they have more than one choice.
